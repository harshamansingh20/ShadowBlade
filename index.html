DISHONOURED. REBORN. UNSTOPPABLE.

Once the most feared blade in the Emperor's guard, Kaede was betrayed by the shadow council and left for dead in the burning ruins of Ashenvale. But death refused him. Resurrected by an ancient spirit bound to his bloodline, he now walks between two worlds — the living and the shadow realm.

Armed with a cursed katana that feeds on the souls of the fallen, Kaede must carve a path through eight feudal provinces to reach the Obsidian Throne and confront the very masters who destroyed everything he swore to protect.
